Festivals of Dinajpur

Celebrating traditions and bringing communities together

Pohela Boishakh

Pohela Boishakh

The Bengali New Year celebration is one of the most colorful festivals in Dinajpur. Communities come together with traditional music, dance, and festive foods to welcome the new year.

Durga Puja

Durga Puja

A grand celebration honoring Goddess Durga, featuring elaborate pandals, cultural programs, and community gatherings that showcase the artistic heritage of Dinajpur.

Eid Celebrations

Eid Celebrations

Both Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha are celebrated with great enthusiasm, bringing together families and communities in prayer, feasting, and joyous celebrations.

Rash Mela

Rash Mela

A traditional fair celebrating the Rash festival with folk performances, local handicrafts, and cultural exhibitions that preserve ancient traditions.

Nabanna

Nabanna

The harvest festival celebrating new rice, featuring traditional rituals, folk songs, and special dishes made from the new harvest.

Cultural Festivals

Cultural Festivals

Throughout the year, various cultural festivals showcase music, dance, drama, and art performances that celebrate Dinajpur's rich artistic traditions.
